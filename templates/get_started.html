Welcome to Our DIU Lost & Found Website

DIU Lost & Found is a secure campus platform designed to facilitate the recovery of lost belongings. Users can post detailed lost or found listings with photos and location information. The system enables efficient searching, filtering, and direct communication between users. Our goal is to reunite items with their rightful owners quickly and reliably.
Join the DIU community and contribute to a safer, more connected campus.
